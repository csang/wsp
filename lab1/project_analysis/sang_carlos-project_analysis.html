Redesign Project for Thunder Island's Website

By Carlos Sang

  • Site Intent
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Targeted Audience

Site Intent

The objective of the website's redesign is to reorganize its content to make it easier for the user to read and understand. Change it to a better visual workflow and have better contrast. With this changes the website should receive more audience. We will also redesign it for mobile users with the objective to make it easier and faster for them to find the information they need.

Competitive Analysis

After some research we found another water park in New York called SplashDown Beach that has a good looking website. We will use this site to compare it and make thunder-island.com look much better than theirs.

Site statistics

Splashdownbeach.com has a three-month global Alexa traffic rank of 1,585,897, and this site is based in the US. The site has been online for more than seven years. Visitors to the site spend roughly 37 seconds on each pageview and a total of four minutes on the site during each visit. Splashdownbeach.com is relatively popular among users in the city of New York (where it is ranked #19,771).

Audience Demographics

Relative to the general internet population, 35-44 year olds are greatly over-represented at splashdownbeach.com. Females are more represented than males and they usually have children with them.

Targeted Audience

Our targeted audience includes families and teenagers. When families are on vacations and have nothing else to do, they will search for parks and find this amazing water park to relax and have fun at the same time. And if they don't want water they can still ride go-karts and play mini-golf. Teenagers in high-school or college would love to have some fun and party at a water park whenever they have the time.
