Extreme Body Sliding

From Proslide Technology of Canada comes the most innovative body-sliding challenge -- only a few others like this are operational in the world today! If you're a seasoned body slider, come to Thunder Island!

Your ride starts with a fast, heart-pounding trip down an enclosed tube. You shoot out of the dark tube into a stadium-like bowl on a sheen of water. There's no control as you rapidly swirl around and around, down, down toward the unavoidable hole at the bottom.

Then you're propelled through the drain (Head first? Feet first? Sideways? You can't control yourself!), dropping through the air into 8-feet of water. Every ride is different!

Thunder Rush is for swimmers only, must be at least 48" tall to ride!

Water Park

Adventure Black Water Tunnel Twisters

The mini Tunnel Twister is dark and fast for all riders. The 365' long with a 65' drop, opaque Twister is an enjoyable ride for youngsters and a fast ride for adults , for those who follow fast riding guidelines.

Body Twisters

Pick up a mat and head to the top! Ride alone or double up and prepare to have some wet and wild fun! Certified lifeguards will be waiting for you at the bottom.

Tube Slide

Grab a tube and head to the of this 360' tube slide. Ride alone or triple the fun by connecting tubes.

Kiddie Mini RIVER

After hours of fun grab a tube and relax while floating around Thunder Islands Kiddy Mini River.

Go-Karts

Thunder Island’s Go-karts are a main attraction for young and old. Challenge your friends to an afternoon in a Formula One Go-kart on Thunder Island’s Grand Prix Race Track!

Practice for a career in racing in one of our three styles of Go-Karts Ride alone or with a buddy! Step into a single or double racer or a Formula One Go-kart and step on the gas! Test your skill – test your courage – test your driving ability – on Thunder Island’s Grand Prix Track! Spectators will watch as you careen around the curves and gain speed on the straight-aways!

Jungle Mini-Golf

Jungle Golf is New York’s largest 17-hole miniature golf course, and is fun for the whole family!

Where else can you play golf with jungle animals watching your every move? Elephants, apes, rhinos, flamingos, zebras, even a giraffe might just give you golfing tips as you wind your way through the shade course. Challenge yourself, your friends, and family, to a game of Jungle Golf – New York’s largest 18-hole miniature golf course!
