Policies

Amusement Park

  • No general admission
  • No alcohol or glass

Water Park

  • Small Children must be accompanied by an adult
  • Under 42" be accompanied on large slides
  • Must be 48" to ride on Thunder Rush body slide. Must be able to swim
  • No food in water park area
  • Everyone must have water band to access water park area
  • Proper swim attire only (No buttons, rivets, belts, zippers, buckles or exposed metal or plastic)
  • No jewelry
  • Glasses with straps only
  • No hats

Go Karts

  • 54" Height required
  • Under 54" ride with 16 year old minimum
  • Go Kart requirements: the driver MUST be at least 54" by law to drive the kart.

Oasis Events

  • Contract and deposit only reserves event.
  • Cancellations in writing required.
  • No alcohol may be brought in from outside.
  • Check with your event representative for food or drink restrictions from outside.
  • Your date is held no later than Feb 1st of the following year for year-to-year events. Deposit and/or contact must be made no later than this to insure reservations. Prices subject to change for catered events.

Inclement Weather Policy

NYS law requires closing water attractions for 30 minutes following any lightening in the area.

Closing more than 40 minutes: Any guest who does not wish to stay can receive a coupon for $3.00 off a Park Pass, good any day this season.

Closing more than 60 minutes: A rain check, good any day this season may be given to departing guests.

Closing more than 90 minutes: Slides may close for the day and guests will receive a rain check good any day.
